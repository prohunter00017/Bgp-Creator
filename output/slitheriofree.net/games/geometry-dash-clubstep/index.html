Mastering the Basics

In Geometry Dash Clubstep, you control your icon using the left-click button, the spacebar, or the up arrow key. Whether you’re a cube, a ball, a UFO, or a ship, your goal is to keep moving in rhythm with the music and avoid obstacles. It’s all about timing and precision!

Navigating the Obstacles

Clubstep is packed with tricky obstacles that will test your skills. You’ll encounter faux spikes, fading spikes, and fading blocks. These deceptive elements make it challenging to tell what’s safe and what’s not. Stay sharp and keep your eyes peeled!

Secret Coins Adventure

Ready for a treasure hunt? Clubstep hides three secret coins that you can collect for extra rewards. Here’s how to find them:

  1. First Coin (9%): During the cube segment, look for a diagonal line of three jump rings. Hit the second one to access a gap in a spiky obstacle and grab the coin. Then, slide up and hit a jump ring followed by a gravity ring to return to the main path.
  2. Second Coin (71%): As a UFO, you’ll need quick reflexes. Navigate through an alternate path with closely spaced fading spikes. Jump over three and pass under two to collect the coin.
  3. Third Coin (96%): In the ship section, fly down through the gap in the first demon construction and collect the coin after regaining control. Gathering all three coins and completing the level will reward you with an additional UFO!
