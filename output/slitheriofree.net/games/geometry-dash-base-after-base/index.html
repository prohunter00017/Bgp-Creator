Overview

Welcome, young explorers, to the thrilling world of geometry-dash-base-after-base! This level is a super fun and challenging twist on the original "Base After Base" level in Geometry Dash. With a Harder rating of 6 stars, it's packed with fast-paced action, tricky obstacles, and secret coins just waiting to be discovered. Are you ready to dive in and unlock all the cool secrets?

How to Play

To start your adventure, use the [up] arrow, [w] key, [spacebar], or simply click to jump and reach the yellow rings. Watch out for those spikes! When you're in ship mode, hold the button to fly upward and release to descend. Jump higher by hitting a yellow pad, or go even higher with a blue pad (except when you're in a gravity portal). Don't forget to collect coins for bonus points. If you need to reduce lag, press L to toggle effects.

Unlocking Hidden Coins

Hidden coins add an extra layer of excitement to your journey. Here are some tips to help you find them:

  • At 22%: Land precisely on a jump ring above three spikes. Slide down two levels to find an exposed column that leads to the first hidden coin and a jumping pad.
  • At 47%: Dodge two spikes overhead and jump to the next platform. Leap over a spike and find a gravity portal below. Enter the portal to continue.
  • At 67%: The third hidden coin is at the end of a tunnel. Fly through a narrow gap, avoiding three dangerous spikes to claim the coin.

Each hidden coin you find will make your adventure even more rewarding. So, keep your eyes peeled and your fingers ready to jump, fly, and dodge your way to victory in geometry-dash-base-after-base!
